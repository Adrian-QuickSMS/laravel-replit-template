Section Definitions
1. Campaign Details
Captures campaign identification metadata.
Includes:
•	Campaign Name
Purpose:
•	Reporting
•	Audit
•	Campaign history
•	Message exports
________________________________________
2. Channel & Sender
Defines how the message is delivered and who it appears to come from.
Includes:
•	Channel selection:
o	SMS
o	Basic RCS with SMS fallback
o	Rich RCS with SMS fallback
•	SMS SenderID selection
•	RCS Agent selection (with brand logo)
Purpose:
•	Operator compliance
•	Brand clarity
•	Correct routing and fallback behaviour
________________________________________
3. Recipients
Defines who will receive the message.
Includes:
•	Manual number entry
•	CSV / Excel upload
•	Contact Book selector:
o	Contacts
o	Lists
o	Dynamic Lists
o	Tags
•	Validation and de-duplication logic
•	Real-time recipient summary
Purpose:
•	Accurate audience definition
•	Prevention of duplicate or invalid sends
________________________________________
4. Content
Consolidated message configuration area.
Includes:
•	Message template selection
•	SMS content editor (used by all channels)
•	RCS rich content editor (Rich RCS only):
o	Cards
o	Images
o	Buttons
•	Scheduling options
•	Sending window / unsociable hours
•	Tracking URL insertion
Purpose:
•	Single cohesive authoring area
•	Reduced fragmentation
•	Improved usability and scanability
________________________________________
5. Opt-out Management
Explicit compliance-focused section.
Includes:
•	Opt-out list selection
•	Exclusion rules
•	Compliance messaging
Purpose:
•	Clear regulatory separation
•	Reduced risk of non-compliance
•	Explicit, auditable opt-out behaviour
________________________________________
6. Message Preview (Persistent)
A fixed or sticky preview panel that is always visible on screen.
Includes:
•	SMS preview
•	RCS preview (context-aware)
•	SenderID and RCS agent branding
•	Button rendering (Rich RCS only)
Behaviour:
•	Updates in real time
•	Cannot be fully collapsed
•	Reflects actual delivery channel used
Purpose:
•	Immediate feedback
•	Reduced errors
•	Faster iteration
•	Higher sender confidence
