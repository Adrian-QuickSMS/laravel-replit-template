Why this fixes the height gap
The old design used .row > .col-lg-8 > .card wrappers inside the tab-pane. Bootstrap's .row is display: flex which interacts with SmartWizard's .tab-content height management, causing the first card to stretch to fill the container. The new design is a flat <table> directly inside the tab-pane with no flex containers, so it renders at its natural content height.

Do NOT change any JavaScript - the element IDs (reviewAccount, reviewApiName, etc.) are all the same so existing JS will work as-is.
